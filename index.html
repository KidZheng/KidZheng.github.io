Bio

I am an incoming PhD student at MIT, advised by Frédo Durand and Bill Freeman. During my undergraduate study at UT Austin, I was fortunate to work with Philipp Krähenbühl, Katie Bouman, and Yisong Yue on 3D perception and accelerated MRI. My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision (both low and high-level) and imaging (RGB and beyond).
